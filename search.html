Why Buy Private And Personalised Registrations From Swiftreg

Swiftreg are a recognised reseller of DVLA registrations and offer over 50 million great value number plates. Use our search facility above to find your perfect registration today!

Why not choose our Managed Transfer for a 'Hassle Free' service from the moment you purchase your registration to getting it assigned to your chosen vehicle! For a small fee, we will handle the full legal process and get the registration on your vehicle as soon as possible. We are also a registered number plate supplier (RNPS) and can supply high quality and road legal number plates with your purchase.

You can purchase all registrations using a debit or credit card, or split the purchase over 6/12/24 months! (upto a maximum value of £4000).

Our team are on hand to assist with the full registration process, and can be contacted on 01732 280212.
