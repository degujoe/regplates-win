Make Your Own Number Plate

Use Our Search To Make Your Personalized Registration

Use our advanced search options below to find your ideal Current Style or Prefix Style number plate. You can enter any combination of letters/numbers to find all matching registrations with ease, accessing the millions of registrations we have listed in our database.

If you are stuck for ideas on creating your perfect registration, popular searches usually include names (either first or surname), initials, company names, nicknames, hobbies, favourite sports teams, lucky numbers, car make and models, colours and more!.

As you search, our site will showcase all the best matching registrations. Want more choice? Leave some letters or numbers blank and our site will show you all variations available.
