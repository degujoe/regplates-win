Question About Buying This Private Plate

Can I assign BEN 829V to my vehicle?
This registration BEN 829V can only be transferred onto a vehicle registered on or after August 1979

DVLA specify that you cannot make your vehicle appear newer than it really is. For example, you cannot assign a registration issued in 2012 onto a vehicle registered in 2005. 

The vehicle must also require tax, be MOT testable (not exempt) and not a 'Q' plate.
I do not have a car, can I still buy now?
If you don't have a car yet, you can still purchase the registration today, and assign it at a later date! The registration BEN 829V will be issued on a certificate until you are ready to assign to a suitable vehicle

Can I buy this registration as a gift?
You sure can!  If you are purchasing this registration as a gift, you can gift the recipient the certificate for them to assign  either straight away or at a later date!

The only information we require is the name of the person that the vehicle is registered to. You can tell us this during the checkout process. 
Do I need to transfer BEN 829V to a vehicle immediately
No, you can either assign it straight away or keep on retention until until you are ready to assign.

Please note: The DVLA transfer fee is always payable at the point of purchase, but you will not be charged again when you assign your registration mark at a later date.

What information do I require to purchase this BEN 829V?
We only require the keepers name of the vehicle you wish to transfer this registration onto. You can enter this during the checkout process under the "Nominee" section.
